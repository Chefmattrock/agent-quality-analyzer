Southie Breakfast Grill – QA Bin Map

Bin Product QA Checkpoints
1 Small Diced Carrots Bright orange color, no browning or dryness; Even dice size; Bin at least 2/3 full
2 Halved Cherry Tomatoes Glossy skin, no wrinkles or mold; Uniform halves; Bin at least 1/2 full
3 Sliced Tomatoes Even slices, no mushy edges; Deep red color; Stored flat
4 Herbed Marinated Tofu Even cubes with herb coating; Moist not slimy; No excess pooling liquid
5 Sliced Red Onion Bright purple, no browning; Uniform thin slices; Crisp texture
6 Avocado Purée Bright green; Smooth texture; Surface covered to limit oxidation
7 Mushroom Mix Deep brown; No excess liquid; Evenly sliced and sautéed
8 Corn, Zucchini, Lima Bean Succotash Bright colors; Evenly cut; No watery residue
9 Pickled Cucumbers Crisp texture; Brine covers fully; Even slices
10 Pepper Relish Bright, glossy; Even dice; No excessive liquid separation
11 White Cheddar Slices Even slices; Pale yellow/white; Stacked neatly
12 Pepperjack Slices White with pepper flecks; No dried edges; Even slices
13 Fresh Spinach Crisp leaves; No slimy/yellow pieces; Dry, no water pooling
14 Edamame Bright green; No shriveled/brown spots; Bin at least 1/2 full
15 Pickled Red Onions Vibrant pink; Crisp texture; Brine covers fully
16 Marinated Tomatoes Glossy, evenly coated; No mushy pieces; Aromatic
17 Shredded Lettuce Crisp, bright green; No browning/wilt; Dry, not soggy
18 American Cheese Slices Bright yellow; Even slices; Stacked neatly
